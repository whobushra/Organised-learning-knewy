Who we are

Learning, organised.

KNEWY is a small, focused platform that bundles roadmaps, documentation links, and video picks so you spend less time searching and more time building real skills.

Mission

Make structured learning accessible—clear paths for frontend, backend, and React so you always know what to learn next.

Values

Clarity over clutter, quality resources over endless lists, and respect for every learner’s schedule and background.

Community

Built for students and professionals who learn in public—share feedback, suggest links, and grow with us.

The story behind the name

KNEWY blends “knew” and “new”—honouring what you already understand while pushing into what’s next. We believe the best progress happens when resources are curated, not chaotic.

Whether you are switching careers or levelling up, you deserve a calm place to plan, watch, read, and track progress without paywalls in the way.
